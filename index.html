Events

Our Student Branch has hosted many events, competitions, workshops and interactive sessions in the past 5 years. Benchmark events are events in which we see participation in huge numbers and that students are enjoying to their fullest, so we turn those events into yearly repeating events. Our Current Benchmark Events are the following.

Decrypton

Encrypton based cipher puzzles

Our first benchmark event, established in the year 2015. Participation of 200+ students which is increasing every iteration

Code Hunt

Programming based treasure hunt

Our Second Benchmark Event, established in the year 2016. This was one of our first inter-college events open to outside participation.

Quiz-O-Mania

Tech Based Game Show

Our latest Benchmark Event, established in the year 2017. First of its kind, game-show style event with a host, buzzers and rapid-fire rounds.

About Us

What our Student Branch Stands for and What We Do


Our Student branch has been working actively for past many years. We are a University Level Branch at Amity University Noida campus, conducting many events, workshops, seminars, interactive lectures, hands-on sessions, competitions etc. Amity CSI Student Branch comes under the Region-I of the National Level CSI Division.
